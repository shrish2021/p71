whats new

  • Wearing masks

    Because of COVID-19, Ford Elementary wants all of us to wear at least 1 mask. This helps prevent COVID-19. If we are social distancing 6 feet, in Physical Education (PE), outside, or eating, we can take off our masks.

  • Getting lunch or breakfast

    Because of COVID-19, getting and ordering lunch and breakfast is different because in breakfast we can get a breakfast from the entrance and get it for free, or order it for free, or do both. We all have to be 1 seat away unless you sit with a relative. In lunch, we have to use hand signals to order instead of grabbing the lunch, and lunch is free.
